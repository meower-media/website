Welcome!

Meower is a free, open-source, and nonprofit social media platform free of tracking, ads, and data collection. Join our community today and start connecting with like-minded individuals.

Safe for All

A friendly place to be. We are committed to keeping Meower safe and welcoming for all. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to enforce community guidelines and ensure a positive experience for everyone.

Original

Meower started life as a Scratch 3.0-based social media platform. We have a long history, too! With the continuous support of our amazing community, we've evolved into a feature-rich platform for creators of all kinds. Explore our wiki to learn more about our journey.

Open-Source

Transparency is at the core of Meower. We believe in the power of open-source collaboration. Anyone is welcome to contribute, fork, or view the Meower source code on our GitHub repository. Together, we can build a better platform for all.

Join the Community

Are you ready to join a vibrant community of creators and enthusiasts? Sign up for a Meower account today and start sharing your ideas, creations, and stories with the world. Let your voice be heard and connect with fellow Meowers!
